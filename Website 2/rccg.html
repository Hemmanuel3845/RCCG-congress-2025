HOLY GHOST CONGRESS

RCCG

Redeemed Christian Church of God

Theme: The Great Light

Join us for the Holy Ghost Congress 2025, a time of divine encounter and spiritual renewal.
Experience powerful worship, inspiring messages, and a community of believers coming together
to seek God's presence. This year's theme, "The Great Light," reminds us of the transformative power
of God's light in our lives.

The Holy Ghost Congress is an annual event that gathers thousands of believers from around the world.
It is a time of prayer, worship, and teaching, designed to empower and equip Christians for their
spiritual journey. Whether you are a long-time member of the Redeemed Christian Church of God
or new to the faith, this event is open to all who seek a deeper relationship with God. Join us as we come
together to celebrate, learn, and grow in our faith.
